About Our Campus System

The SmartSupply system is specifically designed for JHCSC Dumingag Campus to streamline our supply management processes. Our solution helps departments efficiently request, track, and manage essential supplies while providing administrators with comprehensive oversight and reporting capabilities.

As a non-profit educational institution, we prioritize resource optimization and transparent processes to better serve our faculty, staff, and students.
