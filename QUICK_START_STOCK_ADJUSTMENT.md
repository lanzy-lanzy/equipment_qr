# Quick Start: Stock Adjustment for Lost/Damaged Items

## Access the Feature

1. Log in as **Admin** or **GSO Staff**
2. In the left sidebar, click **Supplies**
3. Click **Lost/Damaged Items**

## View All Adjustments

1. Go to **Supplies > Lost/Damaged Items**
2. See three statistics at top:
   - **Total Adjustments**: Count of all records
   - **Lost Items**: Total items marked as lost
   - **Damaged Items**: Total items marked as damaged

### Using Filters

**Filter by Type:**
- Select from dropdown: "All Types", "Lost Items", or "Damaged Items"

**Filter by Supply:**
- Select specific supply from dropdown to see adjustments for that item only

**Search:**
- Search by:
  - Supply name (e.g., "USB")
  - Staff member who recorded it
  - Keywords in the reason

## Common Scenarios

### Items Missing After Count
**Type:** Lost Item  
**Reason:** Discrepancy found during inventory count - items missing from expected location

### Defective Products
**Type:** Damaged Item  
**Reason:** Item failed quality check - does not meet specifications

### Theft or Loss
**Type:** Lost Item  
**Reason:** Item reported stolen by [staff name] on [date]

### Accidental Damage
**Type:** Damaged Item  
**Reason:** [Staff name] accidentally damaged item during [activity] on [date]

### Expired Supplies
**Type:** Damaged Item  
**Reason:** Expired supplies disposed on [date]. No longer safe for use.

## Example: Complete Workflow

- Click "View" to confirm entry

- Use search to find similar incidents
- Check if keyboards frequently go missing
- May indicate storage security issue

**Step 4: Reporting**
